Seek is preceded by clusters of watchful eyes and a sharp change in corridor pressure. Once materialized, the entity punishes hesitation more reliably than slow reflexes.

The safe response is simple and difficult: stop looting, read the next opening, crouch before obstacles, and never turn around to inspect the threat.

## Field Notes

- Warning eyes should be treated as a countdown.
- Blue-lit or visually emphasized doors are usually the safe route.
- Sound confirms pursuit state, but visual geometry decides survival.
- The most common failure is entering the chase with attention split between loot and navigation.
